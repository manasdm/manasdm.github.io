Welcome

I am a researcher at the Dalle Molle Institute for Artificial Intelligence (IDSIA), Lugano, Switzerland. My research work is focused on

  • Data-driven modelling and control of non-linear and hybrid dynamical systems.
  • Application of machine learning and AI algorithms to problems pan-industry.

Download my PhD thesis.
